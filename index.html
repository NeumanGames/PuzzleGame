❓ Game Options
  • 🧩Jigsaw Mode — drag and snap interlocking pieces together on the board.
  • 🔢Slider Mode — slide tiles into the empty space to restore the picture.
  • 📐Puzzle Size — from 3×3 (9 pieces, great for little ones!) up to 8×8 (64 pieces).
  • 🖼️Image — pick a built-in image, search Unsplash, or upload your own photo.
  • 📦Tray Position — choose where loose pieces wait: all sides, top, bottom, left, or right.
  • 🔄Rotation (Advanced) — pieces start rotated; you must rotate them to snap.
  • 💾Save Image — heart any image to save it for future puzzles.
